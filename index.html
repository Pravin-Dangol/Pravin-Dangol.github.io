I am a graduate student at Northeastern University, Boston in the Department of Electrical and Comptuer Engineering. I work in the field of Robotics, Artificial Intelligence and Software Development. I am mainly interested in incorporating artificial intelligence with tradition control and motion planning approaches in robotics. More recently, I have been interested in utilizing reinforcement learning in legged robots through hirearchichal manner by utilizing well established nonlinear controllers and state feedback at the low level while employing RL for high level decsions that act as inputs to the low level controllers such as in an impedence based scheme.

I'm currently working at the SiliconSynapse Lab with biologically inspired robots which includes a quadruped, a thruster assisted biped, and a bat-like flapping-wing robot. Recently, I interned at MathWorks (the creators of MATLAB and Simulink) as a software enginerring intern over the summer with the code geration and DDS blockset team.
